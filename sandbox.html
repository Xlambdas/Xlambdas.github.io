The original idea

The idea was born from a simple frustration: feeling like life was slipping by without structure, growth, or meaning. I wanted a way to transform my daily routine into a series of quests, each with its own challenges and rewards. The goal was to create a system that would not only help me stay organized but also inspire me to push my limits and achieve more.
"Game of Life" was created to turn everyday existence into a personal quest for mastery. Inspired by RPG mechanics and self-development philosophy, this system lets you track progress, complete meaningful quests, and level up in real life, you get a visual view of your lifes — all within the powerful environment of Obsidian. It’s not just about productivity. It’s about growth.

Features

The plugin is designed to be intuitive and powerful, with features that allow you to:

  • Track quests and tasks with ease
  • Earn levels and rewards for completing challenges
  • Visualize your progress through a dynamic dashboard
  • Integrate seamlessly with Obsidian’s note-taking capabilities
  • Customize your experience with themes and settings

Who is it for?

Imagine waking up each day with a clear set of quests to tackle. You start with a morning routine quest, then move on to a work-related challenge, and finally, you end the day with a personal growth task. Each completed quest earns you experience points, which contribute to your overall level. As you progress, you unlock new abilities and rewards that enhance your daily life.

This plugin is for anyone who wants to take control of their life, whether you’re a student, a professional, or simply someone looking to add more structure and fun to your daily routine. It’s for those who believe that life can be more than just a series of tasks — it can be an epic journey of growth and achievement.
